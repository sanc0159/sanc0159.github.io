Hello everyone! My name is Verónica Sánchez Guerra, I was born 26 years ago, on the 26th of September in a beautiful country called Colombia. I'm a Libra and I love to guess everybody's zodiac sign... I always fail. I like cats, tattoos, 90s-2000s pop music and taking long walks without a planned destination, I feel most alive when I'm laughing or strongly focused on a task, as I love feeling useful.

Everybody is changing and I don't feel the same - Keane

My hobbies:

  • Art: Digital illustration, coloring, acrylic painting, sketching and watter colors.
  • Old music: I love to listen to Tango and Boleros, they remind me of my childhood.
  • Tattos: Learning about the process and maybe become a tatto artist on the side.

My biggest dream in life is to be on a Disney film production, I wish to create new narratives or scenarios for the whole world to see and feel. This all started back when I was just a kid watching VHS Disney movies with my sister, I would go wild thinking about the many opportunities to change the story. I dream of just creating beautiful film productions with my name on the creddits and sharing them with the people I love and admire.
